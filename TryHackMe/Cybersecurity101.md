# Cybersecurity 101 Notes

Seurity Operations Center (SOC) is a team of cyber security professionals that monitors a network and its systems for malicious events.
Digital Forensics and Incident Response (DFIR) is a specialized cybersecurity discipline combining the investigation of digital breaches with actions to contain and remediate them.
Ransomware - type of malware requiring the user to pay in order to gain access to their files.
